About

Community

Developer Student Clubs are university based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome.

Tensorflow

TensorFlow is a free and open source software library used for data streaming and differentiable programming across a range of tasks. It is a symbolic math library and is also used for machine learning applications such as neural networks. It is used for both research and production at Google.

Android

Android; It is a free, Linux-based operating system developed by Google and the Open Handset Alliance for mobile devices.

Flutter

Flutter is an open source UI software development kit created by Google. It is used to develop applications for Android, iOS, Windows, Mac, Linux and the web.

Google Assistant

Google Assistant (original name: Google Assistant) is a smart virtual assistant developed by Google and announced at Google I / O in May 2016. Unlike Google Assistant, Google Assistant can have two-way conversations.

Firebase

Firebase is a toolset to “build, improve, and grow your app”, and the tools it gives you cover a large portion of the services that developers would normally have to build themselves, but don’t really want to build, because they’d rather be focusing on the app experience itself.
